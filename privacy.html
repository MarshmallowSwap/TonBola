Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This document explains how TonBola collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: March 31, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use TonBola through Telegram, we may collect the following information:

  • Telegram data: Your Telegram user ID, username, and display name, provided automatically by the Telegram platform when you open the Mini App.
  • Wallet address: Your TON blockchain wallet address, if you choose to connect a wallet for payments.
  • Game data: Your game history, XP points, scores, squad membership, and Warrior Pass status.
  • Transaction data: On-chain transaction hashes for payments made through the app. We do not store private keys or seed phrases.
  • Usage data: Which games you play, when you play, and general app interactions, used to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the TonBola gaming service.
  • To track your XP, level, leaderboard position, and game history.
  • To process and verify on-chain payments and prize distributions.
  • To display your profile and statistics within the app.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To send you notifications about game results, wins, and platform updates (via Telegram bot).

3. Blockchain & On-Chain Data

All payments, prize distributions, and smart contract interactions occur on the TON blockchain, which is a public ledger. Transaction data including wallet addresses and amounts are publicly visible on the blockchain and cannot be deleted or modified by TonBola.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party services we use to operate TonBola (database hosting via Supabase, serverless infrastructure via Vercel). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal compliance: If required by applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of TonBola, our users, or the public.

5. Leaderboards & Public Profiles

Your username and game statistics (XP, level, squad name) may be displayed publicly on leaderboards within the app. If you do not want your username displayed, you can choose to remain anonymous in your profile settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, contact us at the email below. Note that on-chain transaction records cannot be deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

TonBola is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the service, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage users to protect their TON wallet credentials independently.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at:

📧 privacy@tonbola.app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the Telegram bot or a notice within the app. Continued use of TonBola after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns:
